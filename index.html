STUDY FOCUS TIMER PRO

System Operations & Guidelines

1. Set Parameters

• Circuit: Choose vector track layout.

• Audio: Select rain or silent.

⚠️ WARNING: Time and Laps values must be POSITIVE INTEGERS. Do NOT use 0, negative numbers, or decimals!

2. Countdown Mode

• Sprint Stint: Traditional fixed focus sessions with rest cycles.

• Auto End: System auto-finalizes and alerts on completion.

3. Count-Up Mode (v0.8-final Special)

• Stochastic & Millisecond: Stochastic pacing (20% speed up, 60% hold, 20% slow down) with full MS display.

• Ceiling Penalty: Hitting limits (Bahrain 1:12, Suzuka 1:26, Monaco 1:09) forces a 1% recovery cool-down on the next lap.
